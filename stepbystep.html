Investing 101

STEP 1:Establish a Goal and Establish an Understanding of Chance

RISK

The first step is to understand what you want to get out of the stock market. Whether it be long-term or short-term financing it all needs to be sorted out by establishing a goal. At this point you should also gauge your understanding of risk and chance. The stock market is capable of mass success or failure. It is dependent on factors that are uncontrollable by the individual

STEP 2:Determine How Much Money You Want To Invest and an Investing Strategy

PLAN

You should also have an understanding of how much money you are willing to set aside and invest. This can be anywhere from $20 to $100. It is important that you accept and understand risk. Additionally you want to be capable of sticking to goals and long term plans as most investment plans take longer than a few days to see growth and development. As do most things in the world, investing money takes time. Being able to develop a way to track progress little by little to reach the big picture is an important factor in developing an understanding patience.
