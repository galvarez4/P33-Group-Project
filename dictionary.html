Here are some terms that are often used in the world of Investments

Stock Market
A network in which shares and stocks of publicly traded companies are bought and/or sold by investors, traders, and shareholders
Asset
A resource with economic value that can be owned and controlled by a person, company, or corporation
Portfolio
A collection of financial investments, they can be made up of stocks, bonds, ETF's and other financial investments
Diversification
An investment strategy that requires investing in different asset types rather than limiting a portfolio to a specific asset type with the intent to reduce risk
Stocks
Fractional ownership of a company and are sold publicly through the stock market
Bonds
A debt security in which borrowers raise money from investors for a specific amount of time
Risk Tolerance
The ability to accept and understand how much money you can potentially lose or gain while investing money
ETF
Exchange-Traded Fund; a type of investment fund which is traded on stock exchanges
NFT
Non-Fungible Token; a digital file that can be bought and sold online utilizing crypto-currency allowing the buyer to own the original file
Index Funds
Investing in a basket of multiple stocks that benchmark a certain index
Brokerage
An individual or firm that acts as an intermediary between an investor and a securities exchange
